## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="What you'll need">
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- A valid email address for account verification
- About 5 minutes to complete setup
</Callout>

## Create Your Account and Log In

Follow these steps to sign up and access the CeresLab platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit CeresLab" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to [https://www.cereslab.com](https://www.cereslab.com).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click the `Create account` button in the top-right corner. Fill in your desired username, email address, and a strong password. Agree to the terms and submit the form.
    
    You'll receive a verification email—check your inbox (and spam folder).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify and Log In" icon="mail">
    Click the verification link in the email. Return to the site and use `Log in` to enter your credentials.
    
    <Callout kind="success">
      Success! You're now on the main dashboard.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, create a dedicated space for your perfumery notes or project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, select `New Documentation Space`. Enter a name like `My Perfumery Notes` and choose a description.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Basics" icon="settings">
    Set visibility to `Public` or `Private`, and pick a theme color (try `#3366CC` for CeresLab blue). Save to generate your space.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create and Edit Your First Page

Publishing content is straightforward with the built-in editor.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a Page" icon="file-plus">
    In your new space, click `New Page`. Title it `Featured Materials` and start editing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit with Markdown" icon="edit-3">
    Use the rich editor or switch to Markdown mode. Here's a simple example:
    
    ````markdown
    ```markdown
    # My First Perfumery Note
    
    ## Key Ingredients
    - Bergamot: Fresh, citrusy top note
    - Jasmine: Floral heart
    - Vanilla: Warm base
    
    > This blend creates a classic eau de parfum.
    ```
    ````
    
    Preview, then click `Publish`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop Navigation" icon="monitor">
    Use the sidebar for namespaces like `Main Page` and `Discussion`. Search bar at top for quick finds. Breadcrumbs show your location.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Mobile Tips" icon="smartphone">
    Tap the hamburger menu for navigation. Swipe to edit pages. Use `More` for history and cache purge.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
