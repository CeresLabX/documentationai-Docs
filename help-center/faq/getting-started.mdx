## Overview

A workspace lets you organize all your project docs in CeresLab.

## Steps

1. Log in to your CeresLab account.
2. Click the "+ New Workspace" button on the dashboard.
3. Name your workspace and choose privacy (Public/Private).
4. Click "Create" to finish.

Your workspace will be listed on your dashboard.
