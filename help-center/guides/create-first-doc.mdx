---
title: Create Your First Documentation Page
description: Step-by-step to create a new doc in CeresLab.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Your Workspace">
    Go to the workspace where you want to add documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Document'">
    Select the "+ New Document" button at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content and Save">
    Enter your page title and content, then click "Save".
  </Step>
</Steps>
