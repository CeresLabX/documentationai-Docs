---
title: Cannot Access Project Documentation
description: Fix for access issues with CeresLab project docs.
---

## Symptoms

- "Access Denied" message
- Project not visible

## Possible Causes

- Not added to workspace
- Wrong permissions

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request access">
    Ask the workspace admin to add your email to the workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your permissions">
    Ensure you are assigned the correct role (Member, Editor, or Admin).
  </Step>
</Steps>
