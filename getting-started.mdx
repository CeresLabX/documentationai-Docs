## Welcome to the perfumery knowledge base

Explore perfumery as both an art and a science, with a focus on understanding how fragrances are created, evaluated, and experienced.

This site covers everything from individual aroma molecules and natural extracts to the people, houses, and brands shaping the fragrance industry today.

The aim is to build a comprehensive, ever-growing resource that brings together technical insight, historical context, and practical knowledge about scent.

This website is in very early stages of development. Expect gaps, rough edges, and frequent updates as new material is added.
