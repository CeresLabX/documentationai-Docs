## Overview

Dell Pro Max 16 Plus (model MB16250) is a 16‑inch mobile workstation built around Intel Core Ultra HX processors and NVIDIA RTX PRO Blackwell GPUs, aimed at engineering, 3D, and content creation workloads that need desktop‑class performance in a portable chassis.

<Callout kind="info">

Specifications, options, and pricing vary by configuration and region. Treat this page as a research summary, and verify final specs in Dell's configurator before purchasing.

</Callout>

## Highlights

- Intel Core Ultra HX processors (up to 24 cores in reviewed configs) paired with discrete NVIDIA RTX PRO Blackwell GPUs.
- Up to 128GB DDR5‑6400 memory via workstation‑class modules, suitable for large CAD, simulation, or media projects.
- Storage scalability up to 12TB across three PCIe Gen5 M.2 slots with RAID 0/1/5 support in reviewed configurations.
- 16‑inch 16:10 displays ranging from FHD+ non‑touch to high‑gamut 120Hz and 4K‑class OLED touch options.
- Comprehensive port layout including Thunderbolt 5, Thunderbolt 4, HDMI 2.1, 2.5GbE, USB‑A, SD card, and optional Smart Card.
- Modern connectivity stack with Intel Wi‑Fi 7 and optional mobile broadband.
- 6‑cell 96Whr battery and 280W USB‑C power adapter tailored for high‑draw CPU/GPU workloads.
- Security and manageability options such as IR webcam, fingerprint reader, Smart Card, NFC, and Control Vault 3 on select configurations.
- Operating system choices including Windows 11 Pro and Ubuntu 24.04 LTS, covering both commercial and Linux‑centric workflows.
- Standard three‑year, next business day onsite warranty in the reviewed configuration.

## Ports and connectivity

The Pro Max 16 Plus is designed to dock into mixed modern and legacy workflows without extensive dongles.

- **Left and rear (as documented across Dell and StorageReview):** 2x Thunderbolt 5 (80Gbps) with Power Delivery and DisplayPort for high‑bandwidth peripherals or multiple external displays, 1 HDMI 2.1 for direct display output, and 1 RJ45 Ethernet port supporting 2.5Gbps networking.
- **Right side and ancillary I/O:** 1 Thunderbolt 4 (40Gbps) with Power Delivery and DisplayPort, 2 USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type‑A ports (one supporting PowerShare for charging devices while the system is asleep or off), 1 global 3.5mm audio headset jack, SD card slot, lock slot, and an optional Smart Card reader slot.
- **Wireless connectivity:** Intel Wi‑Fi 7 BE200 for high‑throughput, low‑latency wireless access, with optional mobile broadband referenced in StorageReview for certain market configurations.

This combination supports multi‑monitor desk setups, wired 2.5GbE networks, and direct attachment of storage, capture devices, and docks through Thunderbolt.

## What it offers (use cases)

For engineering and CAD users, the combination of Intel Core Ultra HX CPUs, configurable up to at least a 24‑core Core Ultra 9 285HX, and RTX PRO Blackwell GPUs (up to RTX PRO 5000 with 24GB VRAM in documented options) positions the Pro Max 16 Plus as a capable platform for large assemblies, complex parametric models, and GPU‑accelerated simulation or visualization workloads.

In 3D content creation and media production, the higher‑end GPU options, up to 128GB of DDR5‑6400 memory, and three Gen5 M.2 slots that can be configured up to 12TB with RAID support help keep large texture sets, caches, and project files local. The 500‑nit, 100% DCI‑P3, 120Hz VRR panel and the 4K‑class OLED touch option described in StorageReview provide display choices for color‑critical or high‑refresh workflows.

For multi‑monitor, docked desk setups, the presence of dual Thunderbolt 5 ports plus a separate Thunderbolt 4 and HDMI 2.1 allows for multiple high‑resolution external displays alongside fast external NVMe arrays or capture devices. 2.5GbE networking and Wi‑Fi 7 cover both wired and wireless environments typical of engineering offices, studios, or labs, while the 96Whr battery and 280W USB‑C adapter support long work sessions and sustained CPU/GPU loads away from a traditional desktop.

Security and OS options (Windows 11 Pro and Ubuntu 24.04 LTS, along with IR camera, fingerprint reader, Smart Card, NFC, and Control Vault 3 on certain builds) make it suitable for managed enterprise environments, mixed Windows/Linux toolchains, and regulated industries that require stronger identity and device protections.
