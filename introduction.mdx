## Overview

CeresLab provides a powerful SaaS platform for creating and managing documentation spaces. You can organize project knowledge, collaborate with teams, and maintain wikis with ease. Whether you document perfumery materials, software projects, or internal knowledge bases, CeresLab streamlines the process with intuitive tools.

This platform supports wiki-style editing, advanced search, and seamless organization, making it ideal for teams and individuals seeking efficient knowledge management.

## Quick Start

Get up and running with CeresLab in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit [cereslab.com](https://www.cereslab.com/) and click "Create account". Fill in your details to sign up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a Space" icon="plus">
    After logging in, create a new documentation space for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Your First Page" icon="edit">
    Use the wiki editor to add content. Support markdown for `code blocks` and lists.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search and Organize" icon="search">
    Add categories and test the search feature with your new content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Here is a simple markdown example you can paste into a new page:

<CodeGroup tabs="Markdown,HTML">
```markdown
# Welcome to My Space

## Features
- **Rich editing**
- Full-text `{search}`

> Tip: Use backticks for `inline code`.
```
```html
<h1>Welcome to My Space</h1>

<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Rich editing</strong></li>
  <li>Full-text <code>search</code></li>
</ul>

<blockquote>Tip: Use <code>inline code</code>.</blockquote>
```
</CodeGroup>
